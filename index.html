Guinea pigs are gentle, social, and playful small pets that love to live in pairs or groups. They enjoy eating fresh vegetables, hay, and pellets, which are essential for their health. With their curious nature, guinea pigs are very active and playful, making them great companions for families. Their average lifespan is 5 to 7 years, depending on the care and environment provided.
